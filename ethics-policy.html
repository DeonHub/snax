Editorial & Ethics Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Diaspora News Guide is committed to responsible journalism, accuracy, transparency and ethical reporting. This Editorial & Ethics Policy outlines the principles and standards that guide our content creation and publishing practices.

Editorial Independence

Diaspora News Guide maintains full editorial independence. Editorial decisions are made without influence from advertisers, sponsors, political organizations or outside interests.

Advertising partnerships and monetization efforts do not affect the selection, framing or presentation of news content.

Accuracy and Corrections

We strive to ensure all published information is accurate, fair, and based on credible sources at the time of publication.

Our reporting relies on reputable news organizations, official statements, public records and verified sources. All sourced content is clearly attributed to its original publisher.

If an error is identified, we are committed to correcting it promptly and transparently.

Source Attribution

Diaspora News Guide aggregates, summarizes, and reports on news from third-party sources. All third-party material is properly credited with clear source attribution and links where applicable.

Original commentary and summaries are written in our own words and are intended to inform readers while directing them to the original source for full context.

Fairness and Balance

We aim to present information fairly and without intentional bias. When reporting on sensitive or controversial topics, we strive to provide context and multiple perspectives when appropriate.

Diaspora News Guide does not knowingly publish false, misleading or manipulated information.

Opinion vs News

Opinion content, commentary or analysis is clearly distinguished from factual news reporting.

Opinions expressed in commentary pieces reflect the views of the individual author and do not necessarily represent the views of Diaspora News Guide as a whole.

Ethical Standards

Diaspora News Guide does not tolerate plagiarism, fabricated information or deceptive practices.

We do not publish content intended to incite violence, hatred, or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, sexual orientation or disability.

All content is reviewed to ensure it meets ethical standards and community guidelines.

User-Generated Content

Comments and user interactions may be moderated to maintain respectful discourse.

We reserve the right to remove comments or restrict access for content that violates our ethical standards or promotes misinformation, harassment or harmful behavior.

Transparency

We are committed to transparency in our editorial practices and welcome feedback from readers.

Questions or concerns regarding our editorial standards may be directed to info@diasporanewsguide.com.

Updates to This Policy

This Editorial & Ethics Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in standards, laws, or editorial practices.

Updates will be posted on this page with the revised date noted above.
